# CareCo Design Planner — Q2 2026

A lightweight Q2 design planning tool for the CareCo domain at Doctolib.

## Features
- T-shirt size estimation (XS → XL) with working-day calculations
- Owner + supporter allocation with split-color Gantt bars
- Drag & drop timeline rescheduling
- Capacity view per person with NL 🇳🇱 public holidays baked in
- **Share plan** button — encodes your full plan in the URL so colleagues see exactly what you planned

## How to use
Open `index.html` in any browser — no build step needed.

## Sharing
Click the **🔗 Share plan** button in the top-right. Your full plan is encoded in the URL.
Share that URL with a colleague and they'll see your exact plan on load.

## Data
Your plan auto-saves to `localStorage` in your browser. Shared links override this with the encoded state.
